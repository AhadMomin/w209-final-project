This dashboard displays the Delivery Status in relation to the locations of the Shippers and Customers. There are two sections in this dashboard:

Section 1: Shippers & Customers Areas

This section shows the shippers' and customers' higher-level geographic areas:

  • Market: Market to where the order is delivered from, e.g., Africa, Europe, Pacific Asia, LATAM (aka Latin American), USCA (aka Central America)
  • Order Region: Region of the world where the order is delivered from, e.g., Southeast Asia, Oceania, Eastern Asia,
  • Customer Country: Country where the customer made the purchase, e.g., Puerto Rico, United States

 

Section 2: Shippers & Customers Locations Details
  • This section delves into the specifics of the Shippers' and Customers' locations.
  • Shipper Countries: Countries where the order is delivered from, e.g., Australia, Cuba, Peru
  • Customer State: State abbreviation for the store where the purchase is registered, e.g., CA, TX

 

Instructions:

This is an interactive dashboard. To focus on your analysis, you can modify the views in the following ways:

  • Select the Delivery Status you want to see by clicking inside any bar chart.
  • You can also narrow your search by clicking on the Delivery Status legend.
  • Click and hold the shift key to select more than one Delivery Status.
  • Click inside the chart to clear your filter selections.
  • Hover over each bar in a chart to see the statistical data beneath the bar.
  • The color of the bar represents the Delivery Status (Dark Blue: Advance Shipping, Light Blue: Late Delivery, Orange: Shipping Canceled, Light Orange: Shipping on Time)

 

Example:

Task: Explore the insights into Late Delivery orders from Europe.

  • From the 'Shipper Market' chart, click on the 'Light Blue' bar under the 'Europe' section.
  • Hover over each 'Light Blue' bar in different charts to view the details.

Observations:

  • 13806 orders shipped from Europe were late.
  • 21147 orders were shipped to the US.
  • France is the top shipper among the Europe countries. It accounted for 3982 late orders.
  • The top-3 US states that experienced from this late delivery from Europe are California (CA), New York (NY), and Texas (TX)
