The two maps in this dashboard show the distribution of the Delivery Status from the perspectives of Shippers and Customers:

Map 1: Shippers Delivery Status Around the World
  • This map illustrates the distribution of the Delivery Status from the perspective of Shippers.
  • Each marker on the map represents a location where orders were delivered.
  • The color of the marker represents the Delivery Status (Blue: Advance Shipping, Orange: Late Delivery, Red: Shipping Canceled, Green: Shipping on Time)
  • Delivery Status: Delivery status of orders, e.g., Advance shipping, Late delivery, Shipping canceled, Shipping on time.

 

Map 2: Customers Delivery Status in US
  1. This map illustrates the distribution of the Delivery Status from the perspective of Customers.
  2. Each marker on the map represents a location where orders were purchased.
  3. The color of the marker represents the Delivery Status (Blue: Advance Shipping, Orange: Late Delivery, Red: Shipping Canceled, Green: Shipping on Time)
  4. Delivery Status: Delivery status of orders, e.g., Advance shipping, Late delivery, Shipping canceled, Shipping on time.

 

Instructions:
  • Hover over any marker on the map to see more information about the location.
  • Inside the map, click on the color corresponding to the Delivery Status you want to focus on.
  • Click outside of the map to reset the filter.
  • Delivery Status legend is not selectable in this version. It will be available in future releases.

Observations:

  • Based on density of the markers in 'Shippers Delivery Status Around the World' Map, many orders were shipped from many locations in Europe.
Based on density of the markers in 'Customers Delivery Status in US' Map, most of the orders were placed from California.
