This dashboard highlights the relationships between the Delivery Status and the key attributes of an online order.

Order Key Attributes
  • Type: Type of transaction made, e.g., Debit Card, Payment (aka Credit Card), Transfer, Cash
  • Order Status: State of the Order, e.g., COMPLETE, PENDING, CLOSED
  • Department: Department name of store, e.g., Fitness, Golf
  • Delivery Status: Delivery status of orders, e.g., Advance shipping, Late delivery, Shipping canceled, Shipping on time.

 

Instructions:

This is an interactive dashboard. To focus on your analysis, you can modify the views in two ways:

(1) Delivery Status Chart:
  • Select the Delivery Status you want to see by clicking inside any bar chart.
  • You can also narrow your search by clicking on the Delivery Status legend.
  • Click and hold the shift key to select more than one Delivery Status.
  • Click inside the chart to clear your filter selections.
  • Hover over each bar in a chart to see the statistical data beneath the bar.
  • The color of the bar represents the Delivery Status (Dark Blue: Advance Shipping, Light Blue: Late Delivery, Orange: Shipping Canceled, Light Orange: Shipping on Time)

 

(2) Customers and Shippers Location Filters:
  • You can enhance your navigation experience by using any of the four geographical-related filters.
  • The Customer State and Customer Country filters allow you to focus on the on-time delivery status in specific customer locations.
  • Customer State: State abbreviation for the store where the purchase is registered, e.g., CA, TX
  • Customer Country: Country where the customer made the purchase, e.g., Puerto Rico, United States
  • Order Region and Market enable you to concentrate on the on-time delivery status in certain shipper areas.
  • Order Region: Region of the world where the order is delivered from, e.g., Southeast Asia, Oceania, Eastern Asia,
  • Market: Market to where the order is delivered from, e.g., Africa, Europe, Pacific Asia, LATAM (aka Latin American), USCA (aka Central America)

 

Example:

Task: Explore the relationships between California customers and Southeast Asia Shippers' performance (Measure by Late Delivery and On-Time status).

  • To view Late Delivery AND On-time orders, from the Delivery Status Legend, click on the 'Late delivery' legend (light blue). Then, hold the shift key and click on the 'Shipping on time' legend (light orange).
  • From the filter widgets located below the page, select CA (California) from the Customer State filter.
  • Also, select 'Southeast Asia' from the Order Region filter.

Observations:

  • You should see 325 Late delivery orders and 106 On-Time orders.
  • 110 orders were Debit payment.
Top-3 departments the orders were ordered from are Fan Shop, Apparel, and Golf.
